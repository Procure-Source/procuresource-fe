Executive Summary

What This Product Is

ProcureSource is positioned as a technical procurement operating system for industrial and MEP equipment. It connects purchase managers, suppliers, manufacturers, agents, and admins around verified product records, RFQs, quotes, contracts, documents, delivery tracking, and technical compliance.

Primary Users

  • Purchase managers creating projects and RFQs.
  • Suppliers managing products, brands, quotes and invitations.
  • Admins reviewing users, products, suppliers and documents.
  • Public users browsing products, manufacturers, RFQs and certifications.

Core Differentiator

The latest work moves the product beyond a generic marketplace by adding a manual trust layer: verification trails, agent authorization mapping, mismatch alerts, response-time evidence, lead-time reality checks, and honest spec confidence.

Strategic wedge: v1 should stay narrow around verified chiller intelligence and UAE/GCC agent authorization before broad marketplace expansion.

Recommended Roadmap

Phase 0

Lock v1 to UAE chillers, build the manufacturer/agent/certificate spreadsheet, and seed VerificationRecord with real manual evidence.

Phase 1

Expose the public certification lookup and verified agent introduction flow to a small group of real consultants.

Phase 2

Only after real usage, expand admin tooling for manufacturer self-updates, verification review queues and quarterly re-check cadence.

Do Not Rush

Do not expand categories, payment workflows, complex RFQ automation, BIM integration or mobile apps before the trust dataset has density.
